Godot Features

  • 3rd person player controller with raycasting in the middle of the screen and dashing
  • Enemies that spawn at a random point in a radius with AI that can chase, dash, wander, idle, attack, and leash back to their original position when too far away
  • Meshes on the enemy and player that are animated
  • Gravity gun that shoots physics objects at enemies and combos attacks
  • Inventory with merging, swapping, placing, dropping a single item, and using or equipping

Roblox features

  • Grappling hook that propels the player forward and uses a rope constraint, counts the swings the player takes and scales up the points depending on what grappling hook is equipped
  • 1100 unlockables including grappling hooks, backpacks, pets, shoes, shoulder pads, hats, different pirate ships you can drive or fly
  • Treasure that displays many UI elements when you pick it up that are animated and an animation on the treasure chest itself
  • Over 20 traps to go with the obstacle courses including a billiards table, eagle swooping down and picking you up, quicksand, trampoline, zipline, wind tunnels, a heart that breaks when 2 players stand on a pressure pad, a door, a volcano, James Bond lasers you dodge, and teeth that chomp
  • Different tiers of shops spread across 9 islands to purchase the unlockables
  • Obstacle courses with checkpoints that rewards crowns for completion

Roblox Freedom features

  • Randomly generated prison that calculates the nearest path between 2 floors and makes stairs, has a teleporter to the next floor, jetpack, speed boost, jump boost, health boost, sword boost
  • Turrets that show a laser beam aiming at the player before sending a fire projectile at them every so often
  • Enemies that hunt the player and pathfind/jump to you
  • Minigames for the jetpack, sword, and zipline
  • Confetti and treasure chest opens once you follow a procedural path to the ending stage
